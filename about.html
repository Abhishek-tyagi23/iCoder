Who We Are

ICoders Technolab is a privately owned Outsourcing Company that provides a wide range of solutions and web services for clients who value high quality service. Our efforts are put towards finding the optimal solution for our clients not towards making them pay for what they do not need.

What we do?

Our primary focus is brought upon Outsourcing which fit best for corporate structures and helps them improve their business. We have developed a variety of client-services like Web Designing, Web Programming , Data-Entry, Logo Design, Brochure Design, etc. But in no event, our clients should think that Impex Solutions is only efficient when working on large and complicated projects. We use individual approaches and can cope with any (or almost any) task. Our development teams are flexible and can be resized and readjusted at any time upon the customer request.

We choose the best for you:

Our activity is built on an outsourcing basis, or in other words, we involve specialized experts who have extensive experience working with technologies, which are planned for project implementation. The use of outsourcing allows us to achieve the best product quality and decreases the cost price due to the involvement of outsourced specialists.

We work internationally:

Even though our main developer center is located in India, we offer services worldwide. Distances don matter for us. Our CRM system allows us to keep in touch with our clients very closely.

Our team of experienced designers, developers ,and project managers are the key to building innovative and customized solutions through extensive testing and your feedback
